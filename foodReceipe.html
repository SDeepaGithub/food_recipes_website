Chapathi

Wheat

Steps:

In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of whole wheat flour and 1/4 teaspoon of salt. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth dough. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 20 minutes. Cook until both sides are golden brown and the chapathi is cooked through
