ChickenGravy 🍰

ChickenGravy is a savory and delicious dish made with mushrooms cooked in a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons oil or butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 large tomato, pureed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Wash and slice the mushrooms.
    • Finely chop the onion, and mince the garlic and ginger.
    • Puree the tomato in a blender.
  2. Cook the Onions and Spices:
    • Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
    • Add the minced garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add the Tomatoes and Spices:
    • Add the pureed tomato to the pan and cook for a few minutes until the oil starts to separate.
    • Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Cook the Mushrooms:
    • Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and mix well with the masala.
    • Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are tender.
  5. Simmer the Gravy:
    • Add 1/2 cup of water to the pan and stir to combine.
    • Bring to a simmer and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  6. Garnish and Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Serve the Chickengravy hot with rice, chapathi, or naan.
