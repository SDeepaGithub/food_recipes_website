Scrambled Eggs 🍳

Scrambled eggs are a simple and delicious dish made from eggs, lightly beaten and cooked until just set.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons milk or cream
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional: chopped fresh herbs (like chives or parsley)

Steps

  1. Beat the Eggs:

    • Crack the eggs into a bowl.
    • Add a pinch of salt, black pepper, and 2 tablespoons of milk or cream.
    • Whisk the mixture until the eggs are fully combined and slightly frothy.
  2. Heat the Pan:

    • Place a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
    • Add 1 tablespoon of butter and let it melt, coating the pan evenly.
  3. Cook the Eggs:

    • Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
    • Let it sit for a few seconds without stirring.
    • Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, pushing them from the edges to the center.
    • Continue to cook and gently stir until the eggs are just set but still slightly runny. Remove from heat a little before they are fully cooked, as they will continue to cook in the residual heat.
  4. Serve:

    • Transfer the scrambled eggs to a plate.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh herbs if desired.
    • Serve immediately with toast, bacon, or any side of your choice.
