South Indian Thali 🍰

A traditional South Indian Thali featuring a variety of dishes served on a banana leaf.

Menu

  • Rice
  • Sambar
  • Rasam
  • Vegetable Curry (e.g., Avial)
  • Poricha Kootu
  • Poriyal (e.g., Beans Poriyal)
  • Pickle (e.g., Mango pickle)
  • Curd
  • Papad
  • Payasam (e.g., Semiya Payasam)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dishes:
    • Cook rice and keep it ready.
    • Prepare sambar and rasam.
    • Make the vegetable curry and poricha kootu.
    • Prepare the poriyal and pickle.
    • Set curd and fry papads.
    • Prepare the payasam for dessert.
  2. Serve on Banana Leaf:
    • Arrange the dishes on a clean banana leaf.
    • Start with rice and pair it with sambar and rasam.
    • Serve the vegetable dishes and pickle on the side.
    • Finish with curd, papad, and a serving of payasam.
