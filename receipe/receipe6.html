Grilled Chicken Recipe 🍰

A delicious grilled chicken recipe marinated with herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, oregano, thyme, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
    • Add chicken breasts to the marinade and coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 4 hours) to marinate.
  2. Preheat the Grill:
    • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    • Brush the grill grates with oil to prevent sticking.
  3. Grill the Chicken:
    • Remove chicken breasts from marinade and shake off excess.
    • Place chicken on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C) and juices run clear.
    • Remove from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  4. Serve:
    • Slice grilled chicken breasts and serve hot with your favorite sides like salad, rice, or grilled vegetables.
